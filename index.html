About Me

My name is Chris Skowronski. I am a life-long student and huge sports fan. I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of Pittsburgh studying Computer Science & Biology.

I love to create and design. I am interested in technical consulting, though I am open to all things technology.

I recently finished up an internship with rhipe Solutions and got industry experience in cloud computing and web development. I created cloud applications and company portals. I really enjoyed working as a consultant and meeting with businesses to create solutions that suited their needs. My main experience is in developing applications using object-oriented languages as well as using Javascript frameworks to build the front-end.

When I'm not in front of a computer I love to be outside playing baseball. Growing up it was such a huge part of my life and I still really enjoy going to the ballpark or just having a catch. I have made lifelong friends on the field and will always be a fan.
